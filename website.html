Maitreyi College is a prestigious women's college in the South Campus of the University of Delhi, located in Bapu Dham, Chanakyapuri, New Delhi-110021, India. It is ranked AIR 35th by NIRF 2020. It was established in July 1967 and named after the Vedic sage Maitreyi.[1] An All-women's college, it offers various courses in the fields of Science, Arts and Commerce. The college was recognised by DBT as a star college, conferring the star status to all four of its science departments namely Botany, Zoology, Chemistry and Mathematics. 

Maitreyi College offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate courses.
